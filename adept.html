Weightless Tread
Tier 1 1 Power

Your tread is light as a feather for one minute. You leave no footprints, ignore difficult terrain, can jump an additional tile and may walk up to your movement in tiles on liquid surfaces such as water before you sink in it. When used, gain Advantage to your next Stealth roll and ignore any Movement reducing effects.

Center Self
Tier 2 2 Power

Spend 4 hours meditating. Choose one attribute — it improves one step for the next 24 hours. This effect cannot raise an ability beyond D8 and the effect cannot be stacked; a second use simply resets the timer and may shift which attribute is enhanced.

Polearm Master
Tier 2 Passive

While wielding a non-bladed polearm:

  • When performing an Attack Action or an Avoid Reaction, roll one additional Fate die.
  • Your staff counts as a Light Shield for the purpose of Avoiding Ranged Attacks.
Flow Like Water
Tier 2 Combat Effect 1 Power
Trigger: You are Avoiding, or a target is Fighting back against you.

Deal base weapon damage. You may move to an unoccupied adjacent tile of your choosing without triggering Opportunity attacks. Then the target must move to an unoccupied tile of your choosing in front of them, triggering Opportunity attacks.
