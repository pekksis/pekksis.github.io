Weightless Tread
Tier 1 1 Power

You may move freely across vertical surfaces and ceilings for one minute. You leave no unusual marks, make no additional noise, and suffer no penalty to movement.

Center Self
Tier 2 2 Power

Meditate for one hour. Choose one attribute — it improves one step for the next 24 hours. This effect cannot be stacked; a second use simply resets the timer and may shift which attribute is enhanced.

Staff Master
Tier 2 Passive

While wielding a quarterstaff:

  • When attacking or contesting, roll one additional fate die.
  • Your staff counts as a medium shield for the purpose of blocking incoming projectiles.
Float Like Water
Tier 2 Combat Effect 1 Power
Trigger: You are fighting back, or the attacker is fighting back against you.

Instead of dealing damage, you flow seamlessly past the attacker's guard. Deal only base weapon damage, shift one tile in any direction, and knock the opponent prone.
