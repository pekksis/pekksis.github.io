Beast Sight
Tier 1 Passive 1 Power

By focusing for 1 minute, you may attune to a nearby animal and perceive the world through its senses. You cannot control the animal outright, but you can project gentle urges to nudge it toward a direction. The animal may resist if the nudge would put it in obvious danger. You experience everything it senses but remain unaware of your own surroundings while attuned. When you return from Beast Sight, you gain Advantage on your next Navigation or Awareness roll.

Berserking Frenzy
Tier 2 2 Power

You fly into a primal frenzy. Upgrade your Might die by one tier for the duration. You can only perform Fight Back Reactions when targeted with Melee Attack actions while under the effect. You ignore any effects that would impact your Might rolls negatively during the effect.

The frenzy ends at the end of any turn during which you did not attack a creature — friend or foe. When it ends, you are Dazed until the end of your next turn. Attacking yourself does not maintain the effect.

Overpowering Strike
Tier 2 Combat Effect 2 Power

You put your entire weight into the blow, kick or slam. Push the target 2 tiles backwards. If they impact with a wall or large object, they take D4 damage ignoring armor. If they impact with another creature, both creatures take D4 damage ignoring armor.

Fight Before Flight
Tier 1 Passive

When receiving Injuries, you ignore their effects until the end of your next turn.
