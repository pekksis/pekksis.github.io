Phalanx Fighter
Tier 1 Passive

While you are standing adjacent to an ally, both you and that ally treat your armor die as one tier higher.

Iron Command
Tier 2 Fast Action

Shout a command to one ally who can hear you. They may immediately move up to their full movement. This movement does not cost them an action.

Intercept
Tier 2 Free Action 1 Power
Trigger: An enemy within 2 tiles declares an attack against one of your allies.

Move up to 2 tiles as a free action, placing yourself adjacent to the attacker. You become the target of the attack instead of your ally. This movement does not cost an action and does not provoke opportunity attacks.

Overextend
Tier 1 Combat Effect

Instead of the standard combat effect, exploit a gap in the attacker's reach. You sidestep or retreat at just the right moment, drawing them into an overreach. The attacker loses their Reaction.
