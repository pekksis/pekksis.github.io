The Knave
Source of Power: Guile

Talents

✦ ✦ ✦
Silver Tongue
Tier 1 Passive

When attempting to bribe, lie, or manipulate a neutral or friendly NPC, you may reroll one fate die. If you fail the roll, the NPC immediately becomes hostile or reports you to the relevant authorities.

Dirty Fighting
Tier 1 Fast Action 2 Power

Throw sand, trip, or shove your target. They have Disadvantage on their next Reaction this round.

Sneak Attack
Tier 2 Passive

If you initiate combat against an completely unaware target, your first attack is made with Advantage and the target cannot spend a Reaction to defend. If you attack a target who is currently engaged in melee with one of your allies, your attack is made with Advantage.

Defensive Roll
Tier 2 1 Power
Trigger: You successfully Avoid an attack or effect.

Shift up to your full free movement. If you end this movement in cover or concealment, you are automatically Hidden from all enemies. If this movement takes you clear of an area effect or environmental hazard, you take no damage from it.

Cut Artery
Tier 2 Combat Effect
Requires: Slashing or piercing weapon.

The target receives Bleeding equal to 1 + the number of Injuries they have already sustained.
