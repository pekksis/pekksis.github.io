The Knave
Source of Power: Guile

Talents

✦ ✦ ✦
Viper's Tongue
Tier 1 Passive

When attempting to bribe or lie as part of a Manipulate roll, you may choose to re-roll one Fate die. If you do so and fail the roll, you are caught in the lie or your bribe is rejected with the appropriate consequences. If you fail the roll but choose not to re-roll, your actions go unnoticed or misunderstood.

Dirty Fighting
Tier 1 Fast Action 1 Power

Throw sand, shove or distract your target. They have Disadvantage on their next Reaction until the start of their next turn.

Sneak Attack
Tier 2 Passive

First time on your turn you perform an Attack action against a Surprised target, or a target engaged in melee with an ally, your attack gains Advantage and a Fate die. If the target has attacked you this round, this effect does not apply.

Defensive Roll
Tier 2 1 Power
Trigger: You successfully Avoid an attack or effect.

Move up to half your free movement without triggering Opportunity attacks. This replaces your standard Movement from Avoid. This movement can pass through tiles occupied by other creatures, but must end in an empty tile. If you succeeded on a 2+, your Reaction is refunded.

Cut Artery
Tier 1 Combat Effect
Requires: Slashing or piercing weapon.

The target receives Bleeding equal to 1 + the number of Injuries they have already sustained.
