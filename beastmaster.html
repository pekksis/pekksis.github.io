The Beastmaster
Source of Power: Primal Fury

Talents

✦ ✦ ✦
Wild Sense
Tier 1 Passive

You are never lost outdoors and can identify any creature by its tracks, spoor, or markings. Once per day, you may ask the GM one question about the surrounding environment and receive an honest answer.

The Bond
Tier 2 Passive

You have a loyal animal companion. It has no stat block but provides utility:

  • Scout: Send your companion ahead to observe a nearby location. You gain Advantage on your next navigation or awareness roll based on what it finds.
  • Guardian: If you are reduced to Death's Door, your companion protects you, imposing Disadvantage on all melee attacks against you while you remain down.
  • Pack Tactics - Fast Action (Cost 1 Power): Your companion strikes in tandem with your next attack. The target has Disadvantage on their Reaction roll against your next attack.
Berserking Frenzy
Tier 2 2 Power

You fly into a primal frenzy. Your Might attribute die improves two steps for the duration.

The frenzy ends at the end of any turn in which you did not attack a creature — friend or foe. When it ends, you are Dazed until the end of your next turn.

Overpowering Strike
Tier 2 Combat Effect 1 Power

You put your entire weight into the blow. Send the enemy flying back 2 tiles. If they impact with a wall, object, or another creature, they take 1d4 damage ignoring armor. You can describe this as a heavy weapon swing, a cinematic kick, a shoulder barge, or a focused burst of energy.
